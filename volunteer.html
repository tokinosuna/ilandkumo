Why This Sanctuary Must Exist: A Personal Story

Have you ever gathered all your courage to reach out for help? You write the perfect email, you check it ten times, and you finally hit "send" to a local support group. They reply, "You're welcome anytime!" Your heart lifts. You feel seen.

So you go. You travel to the address they gave you, your anxiety buzzing but pushed down by a flicker of hope. But when you arrive... there‚Äôs nothing. No sign, no welcome mat, just a confusing building with corporate offices and a loud dance club next door. You circle for half an hour. You send a message, "I'm here," but the message remains unread. The silence is deafening.

That empty feeling of being ignored after taking that huge, brave step? That pain is the very reason i LAND kumo was created.

Our promise is simple: This is not just another project. It's a commitment. Here, your courage will be met with kindness. Here, you will be heard. If you reach out, you will get a reply. We are the difference we wished for.
